Full description: (1476/4000)

Quickly and accurately calculate inductor values with our intuitive app, perfect for electronics hobbyists, students, and professionals.

This app supports four band inductors, and the values consists of 2 numerals, a multiplier, and a tolerance band.

<b>Key Features:</b>
• Color-to-Value Calculator - Quickly calculate inductance values by selecting inductor color bands. Perfect for inductor color-code lookups in electronics projects.
• Value-to-Color Calculator - Enter an inductance value and tolerance to view the matching color code bands in real-time.
• SMD Inductor Code Calculator - Easily convert SMD (Surface-Mount Device) inductor codes to standard Henry values.

<b>Additional Functionalities:</b>
• Share Your Inductor Values - Share calculated inductor details by text or image for easy project collaboration.
• Customize App Appearance - Switch between light and dark themes to personalize your experience.

<b>Why Choose Our App?</b>
• Accurate & Reliable - Based on the IEC 60062 standard, ensuring industry-standard results.
• Fully Responsive - Optimized for all mobile devices, ensuring a smooth experience on any screen.
